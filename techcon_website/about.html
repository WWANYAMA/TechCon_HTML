History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 passionate developers and tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. What started as a weekend meetup has evolved into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually from across the globe.

Over the years, TechCon has witnessed and celebrated the most significant technological breakthroughs of the 21st century. From the mobile revolution to the rise of artificial intelligence, our conference has been at the forefront of technological innovation, providing a platform for groundbreaking announcements and industry-defining discussions.

The conference has grown from a single-day event to a comprehensive three-day experience, expanding to include workshops, hackathons, startup showcases, and networking events that have launched countless careers and partnerships in the tech industry.

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical implementation, fostering innovation through collaboration, education, and inspiration. We believe that technology should serve humanity and create positive change in the world.

TechCon 2024 is dedicated to:

  • Showcasing breakthrough technologies that will shape the future
  • Providing accessible education and skill development opportunities
  • Creating inclusive spaces for underrepresented groups in technology
  • Facilitating meaningful connections between industry leaders and emerging talent
  • Promoting ethical and sustainable technology practices

We strive to create an environment where ideas flourish, partnerships form, and the next generation of tech leaders emerges. Every session, workshop, and conversation at TechCon is designed to push the boundaries of what's possible in technology.

Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential voices in technology. Our speakers have included industry pioneers, innovative entrepreneurs, and visionary researchers who have shaped the digital landscape.

Dr. Sarah Chen

Former Head of AI Ethics at Google and current Director of the MIT AI Policy Lab. Dr. Chen's groundbreaking work on responsible AI development has influenced industry standards worldwide. Her TechCon 2023 keynote on "AI for Good" was viewed by over 2 million people globally.

Marcus Rodriguez

Founder and CEO of SecureNet, one of the fastest-growing cybersecurity firms globally. Marcus has been instrumental in developing next-generation security protocols used by Fortune 500 companies. His 2022 presentation on "Zero Trust Architecture" became a blueprint for modern enterprise security.

Dr. Amara Okafor

Lead Quantum Computing Researcher at IBM and professor at Stanford University. Dr. Okafor's work on quantum algorithms has brought us closer to practical quantum computing applications. Her TechCon 2021 workshop on "Quantum Computing Fundamentals" introduced hundreds of developers to this revolutionary technology.

Elena Vasquez

Founder of Green Innovation Labs and advocate for sustainable technology solutions. Elena has led initiatives that have reduced carbon emissions by millions of tons through smart technology implementations. Her TechCon 2020 talk on "Climate Tech Revolution" inspired a new wave of environmentally conscious startups.
